# Chrome Web Store — Gmail Cleanup Listing Copy

## Extension name (max 45 chars)
Gmail Cleanup — Inbox Organizer

## Short description (max 132 chars)
Scan Gmail, group emails by sender & category, then archive or trash in bulk. Multi-account + protected sender list.

## Detailed description
Gmail Cleanup helps you take back your inbox in minutes.

After years of subscriptions, job alerts, and notifications, your inbox gets
overwhelming. Gmail Cleanup scans all your emails, groups them by sender and
category, and lets you clean them up with a single click.

✨ FEATURES

📧 Full inbox scan
Scans thousands of emails in minutes using 50 parallel workers with a live
speed meter and ETA countdown.

🗂️ Smart categories
Automatically sorts senders into: Jobs & Recruiting, Shopping, Social Media,
Finance & Banking, Travel, Entertainment, Tech & Dev, Education, Health,
News & Media, Newsletters, Notifications.

🧹 Bulk clean
Select multiple senders across any category and Archive All or Trash All with
one click.

🛡️ Protected sender list
Flag any sender or entire category as protected — they disappear from the
cleaning list and are never touched. Persists across sessions.

👥 Multi-account
Add multiple Google accounts and scan them all in a single session. Each
sender is colour-coded by account.

📊 Live scan preview
Watch the top senders appear in real time while the scan runs. See speed,
ETA, and progress percentage.

🔒 100% private
Everything runs in your browser. No backend, no servers, no data collection.
Your emails never leave your device.

## Single-purpose justification (for CWS review form)
This extension reads Gmail metadata to identify bulk/subscription senders,
groups them by category, and allows the user to archive or trash selected
senders' emails. It does not read email bodies or transmit any data externally.
